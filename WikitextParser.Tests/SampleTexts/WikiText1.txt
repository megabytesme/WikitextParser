'''''Mystery Grove''''' is a fictional American television series created by the [[Creator Siblings]] for [[StreamFlix]]. Produced by Fictional Productions and [[First Ave Entertainment]], the first season was released on StreamFlix on January 15, 2022. The second season followed in March 2023.<!--A third season is planned.--> The show is a mix of the [[Horror fiction|horror]], [[Drama (film and television)|drama]], and [[Science fiction|science-fiction]] genres.
